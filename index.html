Instructions

You can use this tool to generate a cold paper wallet.
You should run this file on a computer you trust and disconnect the computer from the internet if possible.
The basic concept and design is the same as bitcoinpaperwallet.com. Canton Becker has included allot of information, tips, tricks and even an instructional video on his website.

Step 1. Acquire A Ripple address

First off, you're gonna need a private and public address to store your XRP in. You can use the button "Generate new address" to generate one or you can use a different wallet generator such as bithomp.com or any other XRP Wallet Address Generator.
If you already have a public key and secret key, you can simply input them in the input fields.

Remember: If you generate an address, test it by depositing and spending a small amount first before transferring more XRP!

Step 2. Generate your wallet

Click the big blue "Generate" button. You should see your brand new wallet appear!

Step 3. Print wallet

The next step is fairly simple. Just click on the "print front" button. Take your printed piece of paper and put it back in the printer feed, printed side up (May differ depending on your printer). Then just print the back.

Ok, got it!
